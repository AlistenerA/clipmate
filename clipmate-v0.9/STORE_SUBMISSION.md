### 简短描述

将网页全文、选区、教程结构和用户选择的图片保存到 Notion 或整洁的 Markdown，并提供本地历史管理。

### 权限说明

- `storage`：本地保存设置、Notion 配置、草稿和可选历史。
- `activeTab`：用户打开 Popup 后识别当前标签页并完成剪藏。
- `<all_urls>` 内容脚本：为用户选择的普通网页提供通用剪藏功能。
- `https://api.notion.com/*`：仅用于用户主动发起的 Notion 保存。

## English

- Name: ClipMate - Web Clipper for Notion
- Version: 0.9.3
- Category: Productivity
- Platforms: Google Chrome / Microsoft Edge
- Manifest: V3

### Short description

Save full webpages, selections, tutorial structure, and user-selected images to Notion or clean Markdown, with optional local history management.

### Permission justification

- `storage`: stores settings, Notion configuration, drafts, and optional history locally.
- `activeTab`: identifies the active tab and supports clipping after the user opens the Popup.
- `<all_urls>` content script: provides general clipping on ordinary webpages selected by the user.
- `https://api.notion.com/*`: used only for Notion saves initiated by the user.
